Welcome to ChessMate chess club(CCC)

ChessMate is a South African leading chess club.The club is a result of a merger between sunnyside chess club and pret chess club in what has been described as the biggest collaboration in South African chess history. ChessMate chess club(CCC) started in 2010 as a gathering of friends to play chesss and quickly grew into top spot in South Africa.
